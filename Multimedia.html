Musician: Rishab Rikhiram Sharma
ABOUT THE ARTIST

Rishab Rikhiram Sharma is a New York-based sitarist, music producer, and mental health advocate. The youngest and final disciple of Pt. Ravi Shankar, he blends Indian classical music with modern sounds and has performed worldwide, including at the White House for President Biden's Diwali event in 2022 and at the Paris Olympics in 2024. Through his music and social platforms, Rishab inspires a global community while promoting mental health and cultural connection.
