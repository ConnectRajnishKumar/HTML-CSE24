DEVI DURGA


Durga, also known as Devi and Shakti, is a principal goddess in Hinduism.
The name Durga means "the Impassable" or "the Inaccessible" and is also a word for fortress.
She is a primary form of the supreme goddess Mahadevi, with manifestations such as Parvati, Lakshmi, and Saraswati.
Durga is also the principal form of Shakti, the universal feminine power.
